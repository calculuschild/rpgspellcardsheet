CASTING A SPELL
If your spell says to do a melee or ranged spell attack, follow the same steps as for a normal attack. Otherwise, your spell automatically hits.

RITUAL CASTING
CONCENTRATION
You can spend 10 minutes performing a ritual to cast this spell without expending a spell slot.
You can only maintain one spell that requires concentration at time, and you may lose concentration if you are damaged.
